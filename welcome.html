welcome to wiki time 🕥

    📖 this extension displays the estimated reading time of wikipedia articles, allowing you to prioritize and plan your reading accordingly

    • its aim is to give you a rough idea upfront of how long an article might take to read
    • simply visit any wikipedia article, and the estimated reading time will be displayed below the title

    ❗️please note that actual reading times may vary as it is calculated using average reading speed of 200 words per minute
